Using FL Studio and Massive, I designed nearly every sound in the song "Turn Off the Dark." This was by far my most complex project yet, and I learned that a crucial part to making a complete song is the small sounds that are mixed into the background. Those kind of sounds gave this song depth and made it sound more complete. Also, I switch up the tone of the song a few times to keep the listener engaged.
