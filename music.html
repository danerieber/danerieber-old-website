Using FL Studio and Massive, I design nearly every sound in the songs on "Inverse", my new EP. This is by far my most complex project yet, and I learned that a crucial part to making a complete song is the small sounds that are mixed into the background. Those kind of sounds give a song depth and make it sound more complete. Also, I switch up the tone of each song a few times to keep the listener engaged.
